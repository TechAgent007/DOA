What Do We Offer?

We offer a multitude of serveices related to a healthy lifestyle.


  • Aerobics Studio - A place for group-led classes like Yoga, Pilates, Dance, etc.
  • Free-Weights - An area for more advanced weight lifters who have a need beyond machines.
  • Machine Weights - Guided weight ligting with instructions for newer or casual gym goers.
  • Health Bar - Healthy snacks and drinks to keep you energized.
  • Stretch Area - Keep yourself less likely to receive an injury by stretching and rolling out your muscles.
  • Daycare - Drop off the kids to get your swole on. We provide light, healthy snacks and activities for children for free for the first 45 minutes of your stay.
  • Changing Rooms - For changing in and out of workout clothes, showers provided.
  • Classroom - For learning more about exercises and nutrition.
  • Nutritionist - A person is available, by apointment, to speak with you on what and when to eat to get your diet on point. A good diet is 90% of the workout.
  • Student Kitchen - A classroom designed to teach you how to cook foods and meals.

We are always looking to expand on what we offer, please feel free to send us a message if you have suggestions.
